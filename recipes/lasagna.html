Lasagna recipe

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types,
of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an
Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with
fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings
and spices.
